Lightskyblue

#87cefa

The <button> hover state background color has been styled using the color model "rgba(135, 206, 235, 0.5)". This color expands the RGB color model with an alpha value of 0.5, providing a semi-transparent effect. The equivalent hex color is "#87cefa", which corresponds to the keyword color "lightskyblue".

Regular 400: The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.

Medium 500: The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.

Medium 500 Italic: The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.

Semibold 600: The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.

Covered By Your Grace

Regular 400: The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.

Text Styles

Body Text

The <body> text is styled in the Ubuntu font family with a font weight of 400 and a font size of 25px.

H1: Main Heading

The <h1> main heading is styled in the Poppins font family with a font weight of 500 and a font size of 100px.

H2: Subheading

The <h2> subheading is styled in the Covered By Your Grace font family with a font weight of 400 and a font size of 63px.

H3 Subheading

The <h3> subheading is styled in the Ubuntu font family with a font weight of 400 and a font size of 32px.

P: Paragraph Text

The <p> paragraph text is styled in the Poppins font family with a font weight of 400 and a font size of 20px.
